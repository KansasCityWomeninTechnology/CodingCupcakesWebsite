The Code

Building websites may not be a common mother-daughter activity, but we're determined to change that.
Mom-dot duos will envision themselves cupcake shop owners in need of creating a website to show of their baked goods. They'll learn website building from start to finishing, doing everything from displaying their own copy to adding a color theme to suit their tastes.
Dads, we love you, but this session is for mother & daughter teams only! It will be a fun, easy introduction to coding, so please encourage the ladies in your life to attend.

The Cupcakes

Of all the local Kansas City cupcake shops, we just can't get enough of Babycakes from the Rivermarket. Flavors of strawberry, chocolate, vanilla, red velvet, and carrot cake are the perfect fuel for building websites.

Kansas City Women in Technology(KCWiT) is a non-profit organization aimed at growing the number of women in technology careers in the Kansas City area. They provide networking events and volunteer opportunities for members, and run programs targeted towards youth to encourage girls to pursue technology.
